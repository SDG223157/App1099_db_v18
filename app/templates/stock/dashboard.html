About Custom Periods

The custom period feature allows you to specify timeframes in the following formats:

Days: e.g., 45d, 90d, 180d
Weeks: e.g., 10w, 26w, 52w
Months: e.g., 18mo, 24mo, 36mo
Years: e.g., 1y, 3y, 7y

This allows for precise control over the analysis timeframe beyond the preset options.
